About Ayodhya

The Eternal City of Lord Ram

Ayodhya, one of the seven sacred cities in Hinduism, stands as the birthplace of Lord Rama. This ancient city on the banks of the sacred Sarayu River has been a beacon of dharma, spirituality, and cultural magnificence for over seven millennia.

With the magnificent Shri Ram Janmabhoomi temple now standing in its full glory, Ayodhya has emerged as one of the most awe-inspiring spiritual destinations in the world, drawing millions of devotees and travelers seeking divine connection.

What Awaits You in Ayodhya

Immerse yourself in sacred rituals, ancient traditions, and an atmosphere charged with devotion.

🕉
🪔

Sarayu Aarti

Witness the mesmerizing evening Aarti on the banks of the Sarayu River, with thousands of diyas illuminating the waters.

Temple Darshan

Seek blessings at over 100 ancient temples, each with unique architecture and divine energy spanning thousands of years.

Experience the grand theatrical retelling of the Ramayana performed with elaborate costumes, music, and devotion.
